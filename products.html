Here you will find some of the various controllers
we have designed and constructed over the years, ranging
from single octave 13 note boards to full AGO 32 note
organ pedalboards, all of different styles and different
types of expression pedals. All of our products include
standard MIDI functions including channel, program and bank
select. Links to the user manuals can also be found below
for selected models.
